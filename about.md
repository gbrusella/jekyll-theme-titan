## About this theme

TITAN pairs a procedural, animated tessellation background with quiet,
readable content panels. The background engine supports several tessellation
models (Voronoi, Delaunay, honeycomb, rhombille, and rotating 3D crystal
lattices) and two motion styles (a travelling bulge and a floating-sheet
ripple).

Set your preferences in `_config.yml` under `titan:`.
